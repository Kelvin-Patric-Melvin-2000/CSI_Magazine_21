The Holographic technology:The new era of marketing

by Aswin Ram

Nowadays marketing plays a major role in the branding of an organization. Social media has made it so much easier for brands to stay ahead of the consumer. However, as this exposure increased, so did the advertising on social media. Frequent advertising to consumers makes it difficult for brands to stand out. Especially during the pandemic, marketing plays an important role in the sustainability of all companies. And also, we all know that India is facing plastic advertising problems and can't reuse the flex for another advertising. What will be the better solution? How to attract customers in each and every advertisement? What will be the future of marketing?

The answer is hologram technology, which will ultimately lead to the future of marketing. Hologram is the revolutionary technology of future display industries like Steve jobs changed entire mobile phone industries by introducing Apple iPhone. Then,the question becomes : What is a hologram?

Well, this is an old theory or light-based physics theory. Introduced in the early 1900s. But emerged in 21st Century

Hologram is simply defined as a three-dimensional image formed by the interaction of light rays from a laser or other attractive light source. The technology behind this roots all the way back to the 1920s. The first 3D hologram image that formed in the world was in 1964. Thereafter, research and theories about the 3D hologram continued for years. The definition of a 3D hologram is that 3D projection exists in space and is visible to everyone without the need of 3D glasses.

Hologram technology has popped up in movies like "Star Wars", "Iron Man"and "Baar Baar Dekho" but nevertheless it's still in its infancy stage only.

It will change the entire display industry. It will be more useful for marketing purposes, smartphone, military, medical, entertainment etc.

  • The entire hologram market is expected to grow from USD 2.7 billion in 2020 to USD 5.4 billion by 2024, at a CAGR of 19.8%. There are a lot of startups and big companies in the world trying to find innovative ways to change the lives of billions of human beings.
  • Big size digital advertising is done by the integration of lot of holograms fan.
  • Holographic design technology is a study area to design holographic visuals. It will be the most valuable employment opportunity for future years.

For marketing strategy, we use: Hologram Fan:

An advanced device used to view 3D hologram visuals. This enables us to see visuals with the naked eye. It's a type of 3D holographic display. Holographic fans are displays that create a holographic image that looks like it is floating in the air. The LED lights (multi colored) are attached to the blades of the fan leaves. It's the best eye catcher device.

The 2 leaf, 3 leaf, 4 leaf hologram fans are also available in the market for different types of advertising purposes.

How are Holograms useful for Marketing purposes?

The main points that defends the usefulness of hologram in marketing are:

  • It's the best eye catcher when compared to other methods of advertising.
  • Eco-friendly
  • Long lasting
  • Can see properly from any side. (Can see from any angle)
  • Can provide Multiple ads in one hologram device
  • Will seek attention of wanted customers
  • High level of customer engagement to know what it is
  • Can understand fast
  • Cost efficient, etc.

Holograms open up a wide range of marketing opportunities because of the potential to identify imaginary content with boundaries. For this reason, we are witnessing a growing trend of brands such as technology and promotion. Viewers of the hologram promotion will share their experiences and impressions with their circle of acquaintances. That is the most important thing.
