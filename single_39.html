IMPORTANCE OF CYBER PSYCHOLOGY

by RITHIKA RAJ

Human psychology is incredibly complicated but we all consider it to be simple. Human psychology has forever been unpredictable. Our behavior and mentality are the outcomes of what we see, read, or with whom we interact. In simple words, your psyche depends on your daily life and your life experiences.

In the past few decades, we humans have been more and more connected to computers and technology. We tend to spend more time in the virtual world and amidst the pandemic, we are forced to stay indoors, and naturally, our screen time increases. As the overlap between man and machine expands, the relevance of human-computer interaction (HCI) analysis can solely increase

Cyberpsychology is relatively a new concept and was introduced in order to understand HCI in a better way. Cyberpsychology is the ﬁeld of study that deals with the mental as well as emotional effects on users because of the web and technology. It is also known as ‘internet psychology’ or ‘web psychology’.

Cyberpsychology helps us to understand how we humans think and behave in different ways while using and as a result of using the internet and technology. Cyberpsychology primarily focuses on the effects of social media and virtual reality on the minds of users. While all the statistical and theoretical research in this ﬁeld is predicted around Internet usage, it includes the study of the psychological ramiﬁcations of cyborgs, AI, and virtual reality too. Cyberpsychology additionally deals with topics that may seem to be science ﬁction stuff, however quickly turning into science fact.

Addiction studies, E-therapy, virtual reality, cyberbullying and online harassment, telehealth, etc. are the few ﬁelds where cyberpsychology is applied. E-therapy is a therapeutic counseling service provided to a patient over the internet and it has always been a controversial topic. Research has shown that the psychological state of Internet users is affected. For instance, their attention span becomes shorter, some get addicted to technology, whereas some are misled or mislead others using fake news and so on.

The number of internet and computer users is increasing at a rapid speed around the world, and as a result the impact of technology on the human psyche continues. Unintentionally, there will be an amendment in our interactions with one another and on our perceptions of the globe that is virtually at our ﬁngertips.

We humans forever tend to blame others for our own mistakes and also the same happens when it involves the internet and technology. We are the ones who don’t know how to use technology and the internet effectively and more safely. Humans have a tendency to alter according to situations but sometimes we get obsessed and forget to maintain the balance we require which in turn affects our productivity.

Ultimately, cyberpsychology research aims to make users understand the consequences of overuse of the internet and technology and ways to use it in a better and secure manner.
