X-STAT is a first in kind hemostatic device for the treatment of gunshot and shrapnel wounds. Unlike traditional wound treatments which will take a lot of time, X-STAT can heal the wounds in a few seconds and stabilize the bleeding until the patient is taken to an emergency care unit.

X-STAT devices work by injecting small expanding sponges into the wounded part by using a syringe-like appliance. After the sponges enter the wounds, they start expanding rapidly and the pressure rise caused due to the expansion prevents over bleeding. The medicinal content filled in the sponges also help the wound to get healed faster.

This innovative medical product is produced by the joint effort of many engineers, researchers and doctors on behalf of the medical device company RevMedX.

There have been many successful uses of XSTAT in military hospital settings. For victims of violence or accidents, control of severe bleeding may prevent shock and may be life-saving too.
