DIGITAL RING-MORE THAN A STYLE STATEMENT

by ANAGHA V R

In this fast-moving world getting mechanized and digitized at a momentous rate is tremendously contributing to the betterment of human life. In every aspect, life is progressing and getting simpler and smarter. From the bulky computers to the handheld version changes happened briskly. This led to the advent of smart wearables. Many updates came which included smart wrist watches too. Hereafter, the human innovations and ideas converged towards miniature smart wearable

The latest innovation, the smart ring also comes under the category of smart wearable. The smart wearable mainly carries out numerous features which include data collection, data storage, and analysis. This new technological progress goes into the account of the researchers of Fraunhofer Institute of Casting, Composite, and Processing Technology. The innovation resulted from the concept of developing a smart ring that could substitute house keys and would also function as a wallet.

What for?

The smart ring mainly houses an RFID chip that will assist in paying bills at a supermarket. It acts as an insurance card. They can be a replacement for key cards. In short, they serve as door keys. Helpful in opening smart doors too. The details like the blood group of the user and other emergency information can be fed in this ring.



What is an RFID chip?

RFID-radio frequency identification. RFID chips can be comparable to barcode labels just because they work similarly with scanners and readers. Their communication with the reader is through radio waves. RFID chips have the capability of simultaneous multi-reader transmission. They can be classified into active and passive chips. RFID chips play a greater part in almost all growing areas. These chips are used as human implants even.

Technology behind?

The ring is the product of 3D printing. As mentioned, above one of the main components of the ring is an RFID chip. The miniature electronic components of the ring are placed in the cavities left behind in the layer-by-layer framework. In between the process of 3D printing, these chips are auto-picked by the robotic system and placed in the cavity provided and sealing is also done which in turn makes it tamper-proof. The chip is affixed in such a manner that the signals have to penetrate just 1mm of the metal. To study the uninterrupted passage of EM signals from the chip through the metal the researchers used frequency of signals in the range of 120kHz. Senior researcher Binder explained that the signals are less effectively shielded by the metal used in making the ring.

The method implemented in the process of manufacturing opens wider opportunities in the 3D printing area. Integrating the electronic components in between the process of 3D printing was once considered difficult, with the production of these smart rings questions regarding that can be addressed.

These innovations in the coming future will open wider opportunities for further research works. From the very first widely available smartwatch by Samsung, the list of smart wearables with newer technologies incorporated brings new dimensions even in the style statement of humans. The transition from each stage is a remarkable milestone. To the family of wearable computers, smartwatch, pulse watch here comes a new member: a smart ring. Let this magic ring redefine the future technological notions.
