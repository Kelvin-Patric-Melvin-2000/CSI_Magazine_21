PADRONE RING

by APARNA S

A Padrone ring is a magic ring that gives the user the power to control a PC. This stunning innovation was created by three founders-Mark Speck, Nicholas Richtil and Thomas Stauffer to bring out new user experience. The degree of innovativeness of the mouse has fascinated several potential users and also the media such as Forbes, which mentioned Padrone as one of the 22 innovative start-ups to watch for CES Las Vegas, Gizmondo, Cnet,Wired among others. Padrone is also in the Innosuisse coaching program.

Padrone is the coolest PC mouse ever. It is extremely wonderful and simple, works just like a PC mouse; however, it is wearable and deals with our fingertips. Featuring a stylish design, this device modernizes the way we interact with the computer.

Padrone follows index finger movement, turning any work desk into a limitless touchpad. The index finger on which the ring sits is used to tap and move the cursor, the middle finger activates left click while both the fingers together facilitate scrolling. No software installation is required and it is connected to the computer via Bluetooth and is equipped with multiple sensors to track the movements of fingers. It takes less than three hours to charge with the wireless charging station.

They are available in 12 different sizes and weigh only 8 grams. More importantly, they're waterproof which means the user can wear it while washing the hands too.
