Robotics Today- A series of technical talks is a virtual robotics seminar series. The goal of the series is to bring the robotics community together during this challenging time. A simpler, more complete definition of robotic types can be narrowed down to fine types - cartesian, cylindrical, SCARA, 6-Axis and delta. Each industrial robot type has specific elements that make them best-suited for different applications. The main differentiators among them are their speed, size and workspace.

Robotics is a branch of mechanical engineering, electrical engineering and computer science that deals with the design, construction, operation and application of robots, as well as computer systems for their control sensory feedback and information processing.
