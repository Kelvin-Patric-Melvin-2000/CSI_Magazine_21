CANVA : A WOMEN'S ENDEAVOUR TO BEAUTIFY THE WORLD AROUND

by GOUTHAM RAJESH

Before starting this article, I would like to note that Women’s encouragement is the fuel for women’s empowerment more than women’s reservation when it comes to the domain of Technology.

A lady has found her birth mother with the help of a poster that she posted on Facebook and you came to know that it was because of your template. Similarly, Canva also has its own story that encourages them daily to go beyond their limits and take over the Internet World’s Design. From getting rejected by the Tech tycoon’s valley- The Silicon Valley, she and her boyfriend took over an oath to make their dream come true.

Highlight

Separated they live in Bookmarksgrove right at the coast of the Semantics, a large language ocean. A small river named Duden flows by their place and supplies it with the necessary regelialia

“IF WE CONTINUE TO DEVELOP OUR TECHNOLOGY, WITHOUT WISDOM OR PRUDENCE, OUR SERVANT MAY PROVE TO BE OUR EXECUTIONER. ” — Omar N. Bradley

Life of Melanie Perkins: Melanie was born in 1987 and was interested in being a skater, later became an early life self - earner by selling scarves at an age of 14. Then during her college days, she also taught students about graphic designing using various idealistic tools of today’s world: Adobe Photoshop and Microsoft Products. She found them difficult for students to learn and analysed that it takes a whole semester to learn the basics itself. There the mixed reality rose and the rest was history. She and her boyfriend began “Fusion Books'' a solution for students to make College yearbooks easily and in a creative manner along with making her college diaries shut down at the age of 19. They found an interesting business element in integrating the whole design ecosystem in a single capsule. This business idea was rejected many times even by the tycoons of Silicon Valley but after 7 years of critical thinking over this concept, she commissioned it in the name: “Canva” in the year 2011. This is just a synopsis of her life, but there are too many convolutions and challenges that she and her boyfriend faced in the way of creating a beautified world.

Being the world’s youngest female tech tycoon at the age of 30, Canva is worth almost 3.43 billion dollars. What makes her stand out in the tech industry? Is it because she is a female? To an extent, it is the main reason. Even in today’s world women’s reservations are a hot topic but still, Melanie proves to us that there are no barriers in the technological domain for males and females. Here all that matters is skill and ambition or the thirst to make a dream vision come true. The technological era is one of the best eras of all time and it is because of its scalability. Here our gender, latitude, longitude, race, accent, fashion sense, CGPA, academic scores, and all such traditional entities that were once considered to be the most important have a lifespan of zero seconds and the only thing that requires is skill and an ambitious mind to pursue a peaceful career in that.

“Let's talk business!” Whether the person opposite to me be Male or Female, I don’t care because the ultimate requirement is the skill to move with courage and a deep perspective regarding the technology and its future. As the brain is something common for all and nothing biased there, Technology offers no barrier for anyone. Reservation is not creating a good opportunity for women but degrading the quality of technology. Instead of Women’s reservation in the tech domain, all we need to serve is a good quality of empowerment from great minds like Melanie Perkins and all. Technology is open for all, whether boys or girls, whoever needs assistance or support, seek and you shall find it. This is the area of advancement, the time is being curved here, don’t waste your time. Get inspired by what is around you and make every moment perfect!
